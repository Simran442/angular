With safer,good-for-you ingredients as its touchstone,lnner Beauty delivers high-performance cosmetics and skincare in easy to use,non-toxic formulas that celebreate ture beauty

After decades working in product development,Julie Rice understood the importence of product performance.When her daughter began reaching for her makeup to play with,however,Julie realized she didn,t want her daughter wearing the product she was creating.Making safer,natural-looking makeup that still performed beautyfully became Julie's mission,and she partnered with a chemist and a naturopathic doctor to ensure each inner Beauty formula was created with only ingredients that were safe enough for even the most sensitive skin to use-and absorb.

What began with an inspired mission has evolved into a commitment to transparency,safety,innovation and products that not only enhance beauty,but protect it from harmful ingredients.Made in small batches in italy, inner Beauty is for sensitive bodies (and souls),everywhere.

Julie Rice
